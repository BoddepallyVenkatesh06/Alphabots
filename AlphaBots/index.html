FAQs

What is Alphabots &its solutions?

AlphaBots is a quantitative investment and analytics firm based in Bangalore, India. We provide retail investors with no-code automated trading strategies backed by quantitative analytics. We also have a backtesting platform, for users to build and test their own strategies.

What is Algo Trading?

Algorithmic trading is automated trading strategies that are based on a pre-described set of rules tested on past or historical data. These sets of rules are based on charts, indicators, technical analysis, or stock essentials.

What is Beta  Backtest?

Backtesting is the process of evaluating a trading strategy by testing it on historical market data.

Alphabots offers a platform with readymade tools to get you started faster, effortless usage procedures.  It involves simulating trades based on the strategy's rules and analyzing the results to assess its effectiveness. Also based on this performance, users can either paper-trade or trade these strategies live.

What is a Proprietary Trading Program?

A Proprietary Trading Program is a program that enables talented and professional traders to enhance their skills. Essentially, we assist traders in developing their strategies and taking them to new heights. Traders can bring their strategies to us, and we will conduct rigorous testing and due diligence to ensure their effectiveness. Once this is done, and the strategy has a performance of interest we would deploy capital in them. Additionally, we could even make the strategy available on our platform and offer traders a profit-sharing partnership.

What is the minimum investment period?

There is no lock-in period for any of our trading strategies, allowing investors to enter and exit at their convenience.

How are trades executed?

On the Alphabots Platform, trades are executed automatically by computer programs that have been programmed to follow certain rules and criteria for entering and exiting positions in the market. We use a combination of automated trading systems and market-making algorithms to execute trades.

Where will your Funds be held?

The users funds will be held in their respective trading accounts. All transactions and corresponding positions are maintained in the client's trading account.
